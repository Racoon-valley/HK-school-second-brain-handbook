The engine

The 7-step self-talk learning cycle

Each learning task flows through seven stages. Click any card to see its dopamine trigger. The cycle keeps you active and produces small reusable components at every step.

Three layers of self-talk (before every task)

First, what is my goal of building this?
Second, why is building this component important to me?
Third, how to build this work? What tools and resources can help?
Small components are the secret. Learners need to build small components by themselves to prove their ideas and reinforce learning. These can be reused in future projects.

AI integration

The A.C.T.I.V.E. learning cycle

Use AI without losing your edge. Each stage keeps the learner active and limits passive overreliance.

The passivity trap: If AI does the thinking for you, your brain won't form connections for long-term retention. Every safeguard keeps your mind engaged. AI as coach, not replacement.
